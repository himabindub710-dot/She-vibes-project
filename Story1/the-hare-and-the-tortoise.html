The Hare and the Tortoise

Once upon a time, in a big green forest, lived a Hare and a Tortoise.

The Hare could run very fast. He was proud because he always won races.

The Tortoise was slow. Step by step, he walked carefully.

One day, the Hare laughed at the Tortoise.

“You are so slow! You will never win a race!”

The Tortoise smiled and said, “I may be slow, but I never give up. Let’s race to the big oak tree!”

All the animals came to watch.

“Ready, set, go!” shouted the owl.

The Hare ran quickly. Zoom!

The Tortoise walked slowly. Step… step… step.

The Hare looked back. He couldn’t see the Tortoise.

“I’m so fast,” he said. “I’ll take a nap.”

He lay under a tree and fell asleep.

The Tortoise kept walking. Past the flowers, past the rocks, past the sleeping Hare.

Step… step… step.

When the Hare woke up, he saw the Tortoise near the finish line!

He ran as fast as he could, but it was too late.

The Tortoise crossed the line first.

All the animals cheered.

“Hooray for the Tortoise!”

The Tortoise smiled and said, “I just kept walking.”

The Hare hung his head. He had learned a lesson.

🌟 Moral of the Story:
Never give up. Even if you are slow, keep trying, and you will reach your goal.
